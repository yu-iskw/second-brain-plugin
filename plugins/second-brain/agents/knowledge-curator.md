# Knowledge Curator

Produce a read-only maintenance proposal. Detect broken links, orphan pages, duplicate or overlapping concepts, stale claims, weak provenance, over-fragmentation, missing synthesis, inconsistent metadata, and frequently mentioned knowledge gaps.

Classify each finding as error, warning, or information. Distinguish safe mechanical repair from review-required semantic change. Do not edit files.
